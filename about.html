Our Purpose

The core purpose of The Benchwarmer is to provide a digital community for students who participate in sports for the love of the game, fitness, and socialization, rather than elite varsity competition. We aim to highlight local pickup games and JV rosters that usually go unnoticed by traditional school media.

Target Audience

Our primary demographic includes high school teenagers (ages 14-18) of all genders. Socio-economically, we focus on free, local public park access to ensure sports remain inclusive and accessible to everyone, regardless of their ability to afford expensive club team fees.
